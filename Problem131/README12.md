Problem statement
Aryan and his friends are very fond of the pattern. They want to make the Reverse N-Number Crown for a given integer' N'.

Given 'N', print the corresponding pattern.

Example:
Input: ‘N’ = 3

Output: 

1         1
1 2     2 1
1 2 3 3 2 1
